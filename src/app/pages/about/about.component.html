About Us

Who We Are

We are built on one principle: The right workforce determines the success of every project.

AGC provides highly trained, experienced, and project-ready manpower across civil construction, MEP works, infrastructure development, and facilities management sectors. Our operational model ensures rapid mobilization, workforce discipline, and strict compliance with UAE labor and safety regulations.

We serve contractors, developers, engineering firms, and facility management companies that require dependable manpower support under demanding timelines and quality standards. Our workforce is screened, supervised, and aligned with client expectations — ensuring operational efficiency at every stage of the project lifecycle.
